Fundamental Theorem & The Sum

To find the exact area under a curve \( f(x) \), we use the limit of the Riemann Sum:

\[ \int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx = \lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{i=1}^{n} f(x_i^*) \Delta x \]

Fun Fact: The "dx" in an integral is actually the limit of \(\Delta x\), representing an infinitely thin width!
